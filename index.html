CASE STUDIES

SELECTED SERVICE PATTERNS, WRITTEN WITHOUT CLIENT-IDENTIFYING DATA

01 // COMMERCIAL ASSET

Commercial Building Rapid Audit

A commercial building operator needed a faster way to understand HVAC performance and prepare early-stage audit evidence without starting a full consulting engagement. Existing information was spread across BMS exports, equipment schedules, metering data and operational notes.

Retragreen used Rapid Audit to organise the available building evidence, review HVAC readiness and identify practical optimisation opportunities. The workflow converted raw operational information into a structured assessment pathway for energy performance improvement and certification planning.

Rapid Audit · HVAC opportunity scan · Evidence review · Audit pathway preparation
02 // VALIDATION TRIAL

HVAC AI Validation Trial

A property team wanted to assess whether AI-assisted HVAC optimisation could support existing operations without replacing the current BMS or disrupting facility workflows.

Retragreen supported a validation trial using RG HVAC Copilot and its analytics workflow. The system helped interpret equipment behaviour, review operating patterns, highlight data quality issues and support engineering discussions around potential control improvements.

RG HVAC Copilot · Trial support · Data quality review · Optimisation analysis
03 // CONTROLLED DEPLOYMENT

Secure Copilot for Institutional Buildings

An institutional building owner required an AI-assisted HVAC support tool while maintaining strong control over sensitive operational data, internal documentation and engineering knowledge.

Retragreen developed a secure architecture approach for RG HVAC Copilot, supporting private or controlled deployment models. The solution helped technical teams query HVAC information, review uploaded documents, support Rapid Audit workflows and retain operational knowledge in an accessible format.

Secure architecture · Private deployment support · Knowledge capture · Copilot workflows

OUR CAPABILITIES

SERVICES

Retragreen helps asset owners, facility teams and consultants turn building data into audit-ready evidence, HVAC insight and practical optimisation pathways. We connect to existing BMS, EMS and site documentation, then package the findings into software-supported workflows.

01

HVAC AI Opportunity Scan

A fast assessment for buildings with existing BMS, EMS or digital building data. We review available system information, identify data gaps and map where AI optimisation or audit automation can create value.

02

Rapid Audit & Certification Support

Rapid Audit organises early-stage evidence for pathways such as NABERS, Green Star, Green Mark, SG Audit and GREENSHIP, helping teams move from scattered files to consultant-ready outputs.

03

RG HVAC Copilot Deployment

Deploy an AI assistant for HVAC Q&A, equipment knowledge capture, document review, alarm interpretation, strategy support and report workflows across facility and engineering teams.

04

BMS, Edge AI & Data Integration

Vendor-agnostic integration connects existing Building Management Systems, gateways, meters and site data. Edge AI options support buildings that need local processing or stronger data control.

05

Validation Trial & Engineering Support

Run controlled trials before broader rollout. We help verify data quality, interpret operating patterns, review control opportunities and support technical conversations with client operations teams.

06

Subscription-Based Optimisation

Continue beyond the first audit with no-CAPEX subscription options, managed service support and repeatable workflows for performance monitoring, reporting and continuous improvement.
